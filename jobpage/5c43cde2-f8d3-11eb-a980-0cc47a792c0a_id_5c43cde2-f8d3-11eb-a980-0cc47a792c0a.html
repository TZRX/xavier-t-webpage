Verse Group is currently working with a product-based company that is progressing into its second phase. Being ASX
listed, they are expecting a massive line of growth in the next year. You will get the chance to work on a product that is
progressive, leveraging, and contemporary within artificial intelligence and computer vision.

goals. If you are a machine learning enthusiastic with all the technical knowledge down packed - this opportunity will
give you a great opportunity to take your career forward.

Assist in the build of a new version of a product.
Mentor other Data Scientists in the team.
Develop and create machine learning modules/algorithms.
Engage and communicate with various parts of the business as well as customers.
WHY APPLY
Work with a small, knitted team which are extremely collaborative.
Work on an awesome cutting-edge product.
Amazing team that will always enjoy a cheeky beer!

Strong experience in productionising models with machine learning.
Strong skills with python.

Experience in computer vision and artificial intelligence.
